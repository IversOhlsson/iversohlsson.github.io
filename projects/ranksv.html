Analysis of CEOs and companies in Sweden

Project Description

The Swedish rank of busniess project performs a dynamic analysis of crucial company metrics over time, factoring in the CEO's tenure and normalized against historical data and industry standards.

Utilizing the DuPont model, the dissect company performance into operational components to derive comprehensive insights into efficiency and profitability:

ROE = (Net Profit / Revenue) × (Revenue / Total Assets) × (Total Assets / Shareholders' Equity)

The analysis spans multiple industry branches and subcategories, such as IT & Telecommunications, Banking & Finance, and Construction & Design, to ensure contextual accuracy and relevance.
