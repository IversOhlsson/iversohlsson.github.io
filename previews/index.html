Each preview ships a real WebGPU shader. Open one, scroll, look at the type, the meta strip, the live status pill, the way the background reacts. Tell me which direction you want me to build the full site in.

  • A

    Aurora — compute-shader curl-noise particles

    ~120k particles, advected by curl-noise fbm in a compute pass, additively blended. Drifting, smoke-like flow. Heavier on GPU, but the most "alive" of the three.

  • B

    Mercury — raymarched SDF metaballs

    Five soft spheres blended via smooth-min, raymarched per pixel with fresnel rim lighting and depth fade. Slow, liquid, expensive-feeling. Reacts subtly to cursor.

    Veil — domain-warped fbm field

    Two-pass domain-warped fractal noise. Aurora-like sheets of soft light drifting across the viewport. Cheapest to run, most atmospheric, least geometric.
